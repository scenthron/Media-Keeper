# 🎬 Video Editor & Media Converters

**This section combines the capabilities of quick video cutting and cropping, along with batch conversion of media files.**

### 🚀 Video Editor
Allows processing video with instant live preview:
*   **Transformation:** Video rotation, horizontal/vertical mirroring, cropping (Crop).
*   **Color Correction:** Adjusting brightness, contrast, saturation, and gamma.
*   **Overlays:** Adding blur to a selected region (Blur), solid color fill, or logo images.
*   **Fragment Markers:** Cutting out unwanted parts of a video or splitting a file into separate segments.
*   **Encoding Settings:** You can control the video encoding parameters depending on your requirements.

### 🔄 Batch Converters
Background processing of files:
1.  **Video Converter:** Changing codecs (H.264 / H.265), adjusting resolution, and bitrate (CRF) with the ability to compress videos to a specific size for social networks and messengers.
2.  **Audio Converter:** Audio compression and audio conversion (including extracting sound from video) to MP3, AAC, FLAC with CBR/VBR support.
3.  **Image Converter:** Batch resizing of images, changing formats (PNG, JPG, WebP), and quality settings.
